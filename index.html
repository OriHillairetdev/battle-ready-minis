Our Pricing – Please Read

All units in Warhammer 40,000 vary enormously in size, shape, and detail. Whether it’s a squad of infantry, a vehicle, a monstrous creature, or an HQ character, it’s best to email us with the details of your unit(s) for an accurate quotation.

If you’d like a quote, please email us with the following:

- Which model(s) you require painting
1. If the models are assembled or unassembled
2. Any magnetization required
3. Wargear options
4. How many require painting
5. Any specific colors/details you require
6. Basing requirements (urban, desert, grass, etc.)
7. Any additional effects (blood, poison, weathering, etc.)
8. The time frame you’d like them completed by
9. Where the model(s) need shipping to

Once we have this information, we’ll send you a quotation and an estimated completion time. If you’re happy to proceed, we’ll provide payment details. Note: We require a 50% pre-payment before work begins.
